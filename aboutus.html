About Us

We are a dedicated NGO working tirelessly towards managing and reducing electronic waste in our community. From organizing awareness campaigns to conducting on-ground collection and recycling drives, our team is committed to protecting the environment. Through education, door-to-door collection, and innovative recycling techniques, we aim to build a cleaner, greener, and more responsible future.
